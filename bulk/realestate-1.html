At SKMC, we understand that real estate transactions can be complex and time-consuming. Our Real Estate Legal and Registration Services:

  • Property Title Verification

    • - Comprehensive title search to ensure clear ownership and legal status of the property.

    - Resolving title issues and providing title opinions.

  • Property Sale and Purchase Agreement

    • - Drafting and reviewing legally binding sale and purchase agreements to protect your interests.

    - Negotiating terms and conditions to achieve a fair deal.

  • Property Registration

    • - Facilitating property registration with the relevant authorities to establish legal ownership.

    - Handling all documentation and formalities for a hassle-free process.

  • Lease and Rental Agreements

    • - Drafting legally sound lease and rental agreements to safeguard both landlords and tenants.

    - Ensuring compliance with local tenancy laws.

    • - Conducting thorough due diligence on properties to identify any legal issues or liabilities.

    - Ensuring compliance with real estate regulations and zoning laws.

  • Real Estate Litigation

    • - Representing clients in real estate disputes and litigation, including property ownership, contract breaches, and more.
