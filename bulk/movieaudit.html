Movie Audit


Welcome to SKMC - Your Trusted Partner in Film Industry Auditing

At SKMC, we understand that the movie production industry operates on tight budgets and tight schedules. Our specialized audit services are designed to provide the film industry with accurate financial insights, ensuring financial transparency and compliance. With a team of experienced auditors and a deep understanding of the unique challenges faced by production companies, we offer comprehensive audit solutions tailored to meet your specific needs.


Our Audit Services for Movie Production Companies:

  • Film Budget Analysis

    • - We thoroughly examine your film's budget to ensure proper allocation of funds across various production phases.

    - Our auditors verify the budget's accuracy, identifying potential discrepancies and areas for cost optimization.

  • Expense Verification

    • - Our team meticulously reviews all production expenses to validate their legitimacy and adherence to accounting standards.

    - We cross-check invoices, contracts, and receipts to ensure expenses are appropriately documented.

  • Royalty and Profit Participation Audit

    • - We conduct royalty and profit participation audits to safeguard the interests of stakeholders and ensure fair compensation.

    - Our auditors verify royalty calculations and revenue-sharing agreements to prevent any potential underreporting or discrepancies.

  • Crew and Cast Payments Audit

    • - Our auditors verify crew and cast payments, ensuring accurate compensation and adherence to contractual agreements.

    - We confirm that all payments are correctly processed and comply with labor laws and union agreements.

  • Tax Incentive Compliance

    • - We assist production companies in complying with tax incentive programs offered by various regions and governments.

    - Our team ensures that all requirements are met, maximizing the benefits of available tax incentives.

  • Financial Controls and Process Evaluation

    • - We evaluate your internal financial controls and processes to identify areas for improvement and reduce the risk of financial errors or fraud.
